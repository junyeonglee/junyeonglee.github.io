### *Semantic versioning*

**Caret**(`^`) & **Tilde** (`~`) in version numbers inside `package.json`

- Patch releases: 1.0 or 1.0.x or **~1.0.4**
- Minor releases: 1 or 1.x or **^1.0.4**
